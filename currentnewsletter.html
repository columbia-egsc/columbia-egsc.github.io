Do not forget to cast your vote for EGSC department representative elections if you are in one of these departments : APAM, CHEM, CEEM, EAEE, ELEN, IEOR, MECH

If you are in these departments, you will receive a link to vote in your email.
Your vote will determine which graduate students from your department will serve as Fall 2015 - Spring 2016 Engineering Graduate Student Council (EGSC) Department Representatives.

Elected members of the EGSC general body are expected to:
  • Serve as representatives to their academic department
  • Act as the voting body for EGSC
  • Actively recruit fellow students to participate as associate members
  • Assist with the planning and implementing of EGSC programs, events and initiatives
  • Attend weekly EGSC meetings
  • Communicate issues or concerns faced by fellow students at EGSC meetings
Online voting will close at 11:59 p.m., Thursday, October 1st, 2015. Election results will be sent out via email to everyone.

 Internship Opportunites ar Pine River Capital

Pine River Capital Management is a global asset management firm focusing on relative value strategies across a full range of financial markets. Founded in early 2002, Pine River has grown into a leading asset manager, attracting top talent and making significant investments in operational infrastructure and risk controls. With more than 400 employees globally, the firm provides investment solutions to qualified clients across three actively managed platforms: hedge funds, separate accounts and listed investment vehicles. Pine River is headquartered in Minnetonka, Minnesota, with offices in New York, London, Beijing, Hong Kong, Shenzhen, San Francisco, Austin, and Luxembourg.

Pine River Capital is coming to campus to interview on October 20th and has posted the below jobs for students to submit their resumes to them on lionshare. They are looking to bring on two IT interns next summer for a ten week program in otheir NY office who will either be rising seniors in undergrad next summer or pursuing their MA degree. Resume submission for on campus recruiting ends on Oct 1 so they are encouraging students to sign up as soon as possible.
 
Position #1
Front Office Technology Intern, Pine River
 
Description
Pine River is seeking an intern to assist our growing firm, working within the Front Office Technology team. In this position, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining software applications to support the various trading desks across Pine River Capital. We are seeking an individual who can learn quickly and has the desire for a career in the computational finance field.
 
Responsibilities:
• Build new tools/analytics for trade execution, research, and portfolio management
• Collaborate with other developers on the Front Office Technology Team to design common services for messaging, market data, and order management
 
Qualifications
Required:
• Rising senior pursuing an undergraduate degree in Computer Science, Finance, Mathematics or pursuing Master’s degree in related fields
• Experience with at least one general-purpose language (Java, C#, or comparable) and one scripting language (Python, Perl, PowerShell, or comparable)
• Understanding of relational databases and SQL
• Familiarity with source control, build system, and testing tools
• A passion for solving challenging problems with elegance
Optional:
• Knowledge of highly-available message queue systems such as RabbitMQ
• Experience developing web-based front ends using Bootstrap, Angular, React, Kendo, or comparable libraries
• Understanding of in-memory databases such as Redis, Ehcache, etc.
• Finance knowledge across equities, fixed income, and derivatives
 
Position #2
Information Security Intern, Pine River
 
Description
Pine River is seeking an intern to assist our growing firm, working within the Information Security team. We are seeking an individual who can learn quickly and has the desire for a career in the information security field.
 
Responsibilities:
• Help research and analyze security event data to identify potential security incidents and next steps
• Assist in providing support for vulnerability management efforts
• Participate in security monitoring and the development of advanced analytic use cases
• Monitor public security advisories and alerts for information related to the corporate IT environment
• Collaborate with other members of the Information Security Team on ad hoc projects
 
Qualifications
• Rising senior pursuing an undergraduate degree or Master’s degree in Computer Science or related field
• Detailed knowledge of security technologies and trends
• Ability to apply very good technically oriented concepts to problem solving
• Ability to communicate clearly both verbally and written
• Work effectively across functional teams
