DGE General board meeting




Please join us for the first DGE general body meeting of the semester.  We will discuss plans for this year as well as positions on the board that will be up for election.  Pizza will be served for lunch.

We will be holding elections for the following executive board positions:
Vice President: assist the president in her/his duties and take over the duties of the president in her/his absence.
Treasurer:responsible for preparing event proposals and the budget each semester
Communications Director: take minutes at group meetings, handle internal communications and manage any social events
Outreach Coordinator: responsible for managing any outreach activities developed by the organization
Social Media & Web Developer: manage the content of the dge.engineering.columbia.edu webpage and any social media developed by the organization.

This is a great way to get involved with the greater student community and participate in great networking and social events as well as professional development opportunities.
