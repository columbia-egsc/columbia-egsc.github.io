Engineering Graduate Student Council

The mission of the council is to:
  1. Serve graduate engineering students as a representative body to discuss their issues and concerns;
  2. Foster interaction, friendship, and respect among students by sponsoring events of interest to them;
  3. Maintain a dialogue with the Administration and Faculty of the School and other schools advocating students interests when appropriate;
  4. Communicate with other recognized student organizations of the University and the University Senate, and represent the graduate engineering student community in these organizations;
  5. Appropriate the allotted funds to student organizations recognized by the Council wherein graduate engineering student participate;
  6. Maintain communication with alumni, alumni clubs, and career services to help students in their professional development;
  7. Disseminate information relevant to the interests of the graduate student community.
